Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about StudyGenius

How does the AI create study schedules?

+

Our AI analyzes multiple factors including your course deadlines, personal schedule, historical performance data, and even your energy levels throughout the day (based on your input). It then applies proven time management and learning science principles to create an optimized schedule that adapts as your needs change.

Is my data private and secure?

+

Absolutely. We use bank-grade encryption for all your data and never share your personal information with third parties. You own all your data and can delete it at any time. Our privacy-first approach means even our AI models run locally on your device when possible to maximize security.

Can I use StudyGenius for group projects?

+

Yes! Our Pro and Premium plans include collaborative features that let you sync schedules with study partners, assign tasks, and even find optimal meeting times automatically. The AI can also suggest division of work based on each member's strengths and availability.

How does the AI know which study methods work best for me?

+

Our system continuously analyzes your performance metrics (quiz scores, task completion rates, self-reported focus levels) to detect patterns. It compares these against thousands of data points from similar learners to recommend techniques with the highest probable effectiveness. The more you use StudyGenius, the more personalized the recommendations become.

Can I integrate StudyGenius with my school's LMS?

+

Currently, we support integration with Canvas, Blackboard, and Moodle through our Premium plan. This allows automatic import of your syllabus, assignments, and deadlines. We're constantly adding support for more platforms based on user demand.
